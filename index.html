    Pomdoro

    You either might know this or have no clue what it is—introducing our Pomodoro timer.

    Pomodoro is a time management method that uses a timer to break work into intervals, usually 25 minutes, followed by 5 to 10 minute breaks. After the fourth interval, you should take a more extended break.

    One of the benefits is that it can help you keep your mind fresh and focused. It is really easy to use, and we strongly recommend giving ours a try.

    Kanban Board

    Are you struggling to keep track and organised with tasks?

    Not to worry. We will be introducing a kanban board to help you visibly and visually monitor the progress of tasks.

    In the meantime, we recommend creating three columns: to do, in progress and complete either online or on a piece of paper. Then, write up tasks you have to do, such as on a sticky note, and place and move accordingly with the respective columns.

    You can also add more columns if you're into the details.

    Remember to take adequate breaks

    Sometimes it can be overlooked or underlooked as a waste of precious time, but you need to know you are also precious, and we are not robots that can work and work. We're humans, after all.

    That is why we strongly recommend you take adequate and healthy breaks. This can be anywhere between 5 minutes to an hour. Do what feels suitable for you and only you.

    Maybe brew some jasmine green tea, cook a healthy snack, take a relaxing shower, or go for a walk with a furry friend?
